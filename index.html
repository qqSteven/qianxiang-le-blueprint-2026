What Chinese Clients Really Need
🔍 中国客户真正的需求
Chinese exporters need more than just a tariff optimizer. They need a comprehensive export process service provider.
中国的出口商不仅仅需要一个关税优化者。他们更需要一个全面提供出口流程服务的服务商。
Capability Gap
能力缺口
Chinese exporters have gaps in their understanding and organizational capabilities regarding many matters — customs compliance, documentation preparation, tariff classification, logistics coordination. This is not because they are unprofessional, but because these matters were previously handled by freight forwarders. Freight forwarders did the work but did not help them build their own knowledge systems and operational capabilities. So when we say "please prepare these documents yourself," they are actually not equipped to do so.
中国出口商在很多事务上的理解和组织能力——海关合规、文件准备、关税分类、物流协调——实际上是有欠缺的。这并非他们不专业，而是因为此前这些事务一直由货代公司代为处理。货代公司虽然做了，但没有帮他们建立起自身的认知体系和操作能力。所以当我们说"你自己准备这些文件"的时候，他们实际上是不具备这个能力的。
Service Positioning
服务定位
Therefore, our service positioning should be: a one-stop export process service provider — from tariff optimization to customs clearing documents, from logistics coordination to compliance consulting. If we only handle tariff optimization and ask clients to handle the rest themselves, most clients will drop off due to lack of capability. Offering supporting services is not an add-on — it is a market access requirement.
因此，我们的服务定位应该是：一站式出口流程服务商——从关税优化到清关文件、从物流协调到合规咨询。如果我们只做关税优化这一块，而让客户自己去处理其他环节，大多数客户会因为能力不足而流失。提供配套服务不是附加项，是市场准入的必要条件。
